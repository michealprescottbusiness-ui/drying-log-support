Privacy Policy

Drying Log is designed so restoration job data remains under your control.

Effective and last updated: July 9, 2026

Summary

Fluid Bloom Media LLC does not collect, receive, sell, rent, or use personal data from the Drying Log app for tracking, advertising, or analytics. The app does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Information You Enter

You may enter customer and claim details, addresses, technician names, room information, moisture and atmospheric readings, equipment records, notes, signatures, and photos. This content is stored locally in the app’s private storage on your device. Fluid Bloom Media LLC does not operate a server that receives this content.

Camera and Photo Library

Drying Log requests access only when you choose to capture or select jobsite evidence, a meter display, a signature, or a company logo. Selected images are stored with the local job or settings. The app does not upload them to Fluid Bloom Media LLC.

On-Device OCR

Meter-display text recognition uses Apple’s Vision framework on your device. Images used for OCR are not sent to Fluid Bloom Media LLC. OCR results are suggestions and must be checked against the physical instrument.

Reports and Sharing

PDF and CSV reports are generated locally. When you use the iOS share sheet, you choose the recipient or third-party service. Any information you share is then handled according to the recipient’s or service provider’s privacy practices.

Purchases

Apple processes purchases and subscriptions. Drying Log reads transaction and entitlement status from StoreKit to unlock purchased features. Fluid Bloom Media LLC does not receive your payment card details.

Apple Services and Backups

Your device may include app data in backups or other Apple-managed services according to your Apple account and device settings. Those services are governed by Apple’s privacy terms.

Retention and Deletion

Local job data remains until you delete a job or remove the app. Deleting a job removes its associated rooms, readings, equipment records, and photos from the app’s local store. Apple-managed backups may retain copies according to your backup settings and retention periods.

Children

Drying Log is a professional business utility and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Security

Drying Log relies on iOS application sandboxing and device protections. No method of electronic storage is guaranteed to be completely secure. Protect your device with a passcode and use care when exporting sensitive reports.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app’s practices change. The current version and effective date will remain available on this page. Material changes will also be reflected in App Store privacy disclosures.

Contact

For privacy questions, open a request through Drying Log Support. Do not include customer, claim, or jobsite personal information in a public request.
